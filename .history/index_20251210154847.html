About Ekwem Ugomma

Ekwem Ugomma is a licensed and skilled Legal Practitioner specialized in practice areas such as property law, corporate law, criminal defense, civil litigation, immigration, family law, and election petitions. She is known for her ability to break down complex legal issues and develop effective solutions, handling every case with precision, professionalism, and integrity.

In addition to her experience in specialized legal transactions, Ugomma is a skilled advocate with a distinguished track record of triumph in litigation and courtroom advocacy. She has represented clients in numerous high-stake matters across courts of record in Nigeria.

With a client base that includes international organizations, she is keen on providing laudable solutions beyond traditional legal strategies, through her ability to combine deep legal analysis with empirical courtroom strategies resulting in commendatory outcomes in disputes across diverse sectors.
